Voting Experiment

This is an experiment on voting. 18 voters like you, divided into three groups, will decide between three options. Depending on the winning option, participants will earn extra pay of up to $2.16 (paid within hours as a "bonus" in AMT). The average pay for all participants will be at least $1.96, and depending on your luck and skill you may earn up to $3.16 in total. We will be running this experiment several times, using different voting systems, but you may only participate once.
